Questions

Where does the price data come from?

Publicly listed prices from Lebanese online retail stores and wholesale distributors. We collect, match, and normalize them — we never invent numbers.

How current is the data?

Most sources refresh hourly. Every data point is timestamped so you always know how fresh a price is.

Do you handle the USD / LBP problem?

Yes. Every price is shown in both currencies, converted at the day's market rate, so comparisons stay honest as the rate moves.

Can I get the raw data?

Pro plans include CSV exports, and Institution plans add full REST API access for integrating Mizan data into your own tools.
